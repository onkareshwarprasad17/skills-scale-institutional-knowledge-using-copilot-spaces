# Change Management Checklist

Template for intake, assessment, and approval of scope-change requests.

## Change Request Intake
- Record requestor, date, and brief description of the requested change
- Capture why the change is needed and business impact
- Identify any affected components or teams

## Impact Analysis
- Estimate effort and schedule impact
- Identify required QA, security, or compliance work
- Identify dependencies and affected releases
- Assess risk and mitigation actions

## Approval Flow
- For minor changes: Product Owner + Delivery Lead sign-off
- For material scope changes: Product Owner + Project Manager + Sponsor sign-off
- Record approvals and any conditions for implementation

## Scheduling and Implementation
- Assign implementation owner and target release or sprint
- Update project schedule and communication plan
- Update acceptance criteria and test plans as needed

## Communication and Tracking
- Notify stakeholders of approved changes and expected timelines
- Track change state in project board (proposed → approved → scheduled → complete)
- Conduct a post-implementation review for material changes
